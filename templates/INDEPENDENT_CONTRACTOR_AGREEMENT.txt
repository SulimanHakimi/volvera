1. PURPOSE OF AGREEMENT

1.1. The Contractor owns and operates one or more YouTube channels, social media accounts,
     or other digital content platforms (collectively "Channels") and wishes to receive
     administrative, monetization, training, and operational support from the Company.

1.2. The Contractor remains the sole creator of all content, retains full ownership of all
     Channels, and retains the absolute right to discontinue this Agreement at any time
     without penalty or restriction.

1.3. This Agreement establishes a non-exclusive, independent contractor relationship for
     the purpose of revenue optimization and administrative support only.

2.2. NO TRANSFER OF OWNERSHIP
     Nothing in this Agreement transfers, assigns, or conveys any ownership, intellectual
     property rights, publishing rights, distribution rights, or any other proprietary
     rights to the Company.

2.3. CONTENT RIGHTS
     The Company does not claim, and shall never claim, any rights, title, or interest in:
     a. Content created, published, stored, or distributed through the Channels
     b. Trademarks, logos, or brand elements associated with the Channels
     c. Subscriber/follower relationships or audience data
     d. Future content or derivative works

2.4. CONTRACTOR RETAINS ALL RIGHTS
     The Contractor retains 100% ownership and control over:
     a. Channel login credentials and administrator access
     b. All content (past, present, and future)
     c. Channel monetization settings
     d. Brand partnerships and sponsorship agreements
     e. Merchandise and affiliate marketing rights

3. CONTRACTOR OBLIGATIONS AND CREATIVE FREEDOM

3.1. SOLE RESPONSIBILITY
     The Contractor is solely and exclusively responsible for:
     a. Content creation, ideation, and production
     b. Publishing schedules and frequency
     c. Editorial decisions and creative direction
     d. Copyright compliance and licensing
     e. Compliance with platform Terms of Service (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc.)
     f. Community management and audience engagement
     g. Brand safety and advertiser-friendly content guidelines

3.2. CREATIVE INDEPENDENCE
     The Contractor independently chooses and controls:
     a. Content topics, themes, and subject matter
     b. Video/post formats, styles, and production quality
     c. Languages and target audiences
     d. Publication schedules and upload frequency
     e. Collaborations and guest appearances
     f. Monetization strategies (ads, memberships, super chats, etc.)

3.3. NO MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS
     The Contractor is NOT required to:
     a. Produce a minimum number of videos/posts
     b. Maintain a specific upload schedule
     c. Achieve specific performance metrics
     d. Follow Company content guidelines or editorial direction

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

4. COMPANY OBLIGATIONS AND SERVICES

4.1. ADMINISTRATIVE SUPPORT
     The Company shall provide the following services:
     a. Monetization management through advertising revenue systems
     b. Revenue tracking and financial reporting for each Channel
     c. Timely distribution of Contractor revenue share
     d. Access to analytics dashboards and performance reports
     e. Administrative support related to monetization optimization
     f. Payment processing and international transfer facilitation
     g. Optional training resources and growth strategy consultation

4.2. NON-CONTROL COMMITMENT
     The Company shall NOT:
     a. Control, influence, or restrict content decisions
     b. Require specific content types or topics
     c. Impose editorial guidelines or censorship
     d. Demand minimum performance metrics
     e. Interfere with creative freedom

4.3. TRANSPARENCY OBLIGATION
     The Company commits to full transparency regarding:
     a. Revenue received from platforms
     b. Administrative fees deducted
     c. Payment processing timelines
     d. Any changes to revenue models or fee structures

5. REVENUE MODEL AND PAYMENT TERMS

5.1. REVENUE ALLOCATION
     All revenue generated by or paid to the Channels listed in Section 2 through YouTube
     AdSense, platform monetization, or affiliated advertising systems shall be allocated
     as follows:

     ● Contractor Share: 99.5% (ninety-nine point five percent)
     ● Company Administrative Fee: 0.5% (zero point five percent)

5.2. REVENUE DEFINITION
     "Revenue" includes all income from:
     a. YouTube AdSense advertising
     b. YouTube Premium revenue share
     c. Platform-provided monetization features
     d. Any other platform-distributed advertising revenue

     "Revenue" EXCLUDES:
     a. Direct sponsorships negotiated by Contractor
     b. Affiliate marketing commissions
     c. Merchandise sales
     d. Super Chats, memberships, or tips (unless otherwise agreed)

5.3. PAYMENT PROCESSING
     a. The Company receives monthly payouts from YouTube/platforms
     b. Within 7 (seven) business days of receiving platform payouts, the Company shall
        calculate and transfer the Contractor's 99.5% share
     c. Payments shall be made via the Contractor's chosen method:

6. TAX RESPONSIBILITIES

6.1. CONTRACTOR TAX OBLIGATIONS
     The Contractor acknowledges and agrees that they are fully responsible for:
     a. All tax filings in their country of residence
     b. Income tax declarations and payments
     c. Value Added Tax (VAT) or Sales Tax compliance (if applicable)
     d. Social security contributions (if applicable)
     e. Any other tax obligations arising from income received

6.2. COMPANY TAX OBLIGATIONS
     The Company is responsible solely for tax obligations on its administrative fee
     (0.5% as specified in Section 5.1) in accordance with Swedish tax law.

6.3. GROSS PAYMENTS
     All payments to the Contractor are GROSS amounts and do NOT include tax withholding.
     The Contractor must handle all tax obligations independently.

6.4. TAX DOCUMENTATION
     Upon request, the Company shall provide:
     a. Annual revenue summaries
     b. Payment records and transaction history
     c. Any documentation required for Contractor's tax filings

6.5. NO TAX ADVICE
     The Company does not provide tax advice. The Contractor is advised to consult with
     a qualified tax professional in their jurisdiction.

7. ACCESS RIGHTS AND CHANNEL CONTROLS

7.1. CONTRACTOR RETAINS FULL CONTROL
     The Contractor retains full administrator ownership and primary control of all
     Channels at all times.

7.2. LIMITED COMPANY ACCESS
     The Company may be granted LIMITED access ONLY for the following purposes:
     a. Analytics review and performance monitoring
     b. Monetization configuration and optimization
     c. Brand safety review and advertiser compliance
     d. Technical troubleshooting related to monetization

7.3. ACCESS RESTRICTIONS
     The Company shall NOT have access to:
     a. Channel login credentials (username/password)
     b. Email account associated with the Channel
     c. Content upload or publishing capabilities
     d. Community post creation or comment moderation
     e. Channel settings or branding modifications

7.4. REVOCATION OF ACCESS
     The Contractor may revoke Company access at any time, immediately and without cause,
     by:
     a. Removing Company user permissions from the Channel
     b. Notifying the Company via email
     c. Submitting a Termination Notice (see Section 8)

7.5. EARNED FEES PROTECTION
     Revocation of access does not nullify the Company's right to collect earned
     administrative fees (0.5%) for revenue generated up to the date of revocation.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

8. RIGHT TO TERMINATE

8.1. IMMEDIATE TERMINATION RIGHT
     The Contractor may terminate this Agreement IMMEDIATELY, at any time, for any reason
     or no reason, with NO PENALTY, NO FEES, and NO RESTRICTIONS by:
     
     a. Completing and submitting the "Termination Notice Form" available at:
        [Website URL or email: contracts@volvera.com]
     b. Sending written notice via email to: contracts@volvera.com
     c. Revoking Company access to the Channel(s)

8.2. NO NOTICE PERIOD REQUIRED
     The Contractor is NOT required to provide advance notice. Termination is effective
     immediately upon submission of the Termination Notice.

8.3. EFFECTS OF TERMINATION
     Upon termination, the following shall occur immediately:
     a. Company loses all access rights to the Channel(s)
     b. Company stops collecting revenue going forward
     c. All outstanding payments to Contractor must be settled within 14 (fourteen)
        calendar days
     d. Channel ownership, control, and all rights remain 100% with the Contractor
     e. Company shall delete or return any Contractor data in its possession

8.4. NO RESTRICTIONS ON CONTRACTOR
     The Company shall NOT:
     a. Restrict or delay channel ownership transfer or access
     b. Impose penalties, fees, or charges for termination
     c. Withhold payments or revenue
     d. Interfere with Channel operations post-termination
     e. Claim any ongoing rights to content or revenue

8.5. FINAL SETTLEMENT
     Within 14 days of termination, the Company shall provide:
     a. Final payment of all earned revenue (99.5% share)
     b. Final revenue statement showing all calculations
     c. Confirmation of access removal
     d. Confirmation of data deletion

9. NON-INTERFERENCE CLAUSE

9.1. PROHIBITED COMPANY ACTIONS
     The Company shall NOT, under any circumstances:
     a. Modify, edit, or alter channel content
     b. Publish, upload, or schedule content without explicit prior approval
     c. Restrict, influence, or interfere with editorial decisions
     d. Impose publishing rules, content guidelines, or creative restrictions
     e. Delete or remove published content
     f. Respond to comments or engage with the audience on behalf of the Contractor
     g. Make channel branding or design changes

9.2. CONTRACTOR INDEPENDENCE
     The Contractor has full, independent, and unrestricted creative and business freedom
     regarding all aspects of Channel operations.

9.3. ADVISORY ROLE ONLY
     The Company may provide suggestions, recommendations, or best practices, but the
     Contractor has sole discretion to accept or reject such advice.

10.3. NO EMPLOYEE BENEFITS

      a. Paid vacation or sick leave
      b. Health insurance or pension contributions
      c. Unemployment benefits
      d. Workers' compensation

      The Contractor is responsible for all business expenses including but not limited to:

11.1. COMPANY CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS
      The Company shall NOT disclose, share, or make public the following information
      without the Contractor's explicit written permission:
      a. Earnings data and revenue figures
      b. Channel analytics and performance metrics
      c. Contact information (email, phone, address)
      d. Personal identity information (passport, ID numbers)
      e. Payment details and banking information
      f. Any other proprietary or personal information

11.2. DATA PROTECTION
      The Company commits to:
      a. Comply with GDPR and applicable data protection laws
      b. Securely store all Contractor data
      c. Use data only for purposes specified in this Agreement
      d. Delete or return data upon termination

11.3. CONTRACTOR RIGHTS
      The Contractor has the right to:
      a. Request access to their data held by the Company
      b. Request correction of inaccurate data
      c. Request deletion of data (right to be forgotten)
      d. Object to data processing

12.2. MEDIATION

      litigation.

12.3. GOVERNING LAW

      Sweden, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

12.4. JURISDICTION
      Any legal action or proceeding arising from this Agreement shall be brought

12.5. LANGUAGE
